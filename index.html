Introduction

I'm a Front-End Web Developer who works with HTML5, CSS3, Sass, jQuery, JavaScript, and PHP. I also have significant experience with WordPress. All of my sites are coded utilizing Responsive Web Design

I love learning and I vacuum up new knowledge. When I'm learning; I'm happy!

I am a self-taught developer. While I built my first site in 1996, I did not pursue it and did not keep up with changing technology. Before moving into Web Development full-time in 2015, for 10 years I was an Account Director for TMP Worldwide Advertising & Communications, an Advertising Agency and Technology company specializing in Recruitment Communications.
